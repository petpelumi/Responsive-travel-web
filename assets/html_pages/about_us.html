About us

Adukaz Travels and Tours provides travel and tourism-related services to the general public on behalf of accommodation or travel suppliers to offer different kinds of travelling packages for each destination. Adukaz TOurs and Travels helps in the procurement of visas to African countries and also several other countries outside Africa. Adukaz Travels and Tours provides student visas, work visas and even visas for tourists. It arranges logistics for travellers upon request.
